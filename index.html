Spider Veins

Spider veins appearance is like spider webs or tree branches and usually develops in legs or face. Treatment is done by Sclerotherapy and Laser therapy.

Varicose Veins

Varicose veins resemble as enlarged and twisted veins anywhere in the body, predominantly in the legs. Treatment includes Sclerotherapy, Thermal ablation and Vein stripping.

Leg Ulcers

Malfunctioning of venous valves, usually of the legs in ankle region gives the condition of leg ulcers as open wounds. Treatment is done by Compression therapy and Surgery.

Frequently
Asked Questions

1. What are the visible signs of unhealthy veins?

Unhealthy veins can look enlarged or bulging, with a twisted or knotted appearance. They’re usually dark in color and may be accompanied by aching or a heavy feeling in your legs.

2. Are varicose veins more common in women?

Yes, especially due to hormonal factors, pregnancy, and menopause.

3. How do I know if I need varicose veins treatment?

If you experience pain, discomfort, or cosmetic concerns from varicose veins, it’s a good idea to see a veins specialist for a professional evaluation to determine if treatment is necessary.

4. Can varicose veins cause leg pain?

Yes, aching, burning, throbbing, or cramping in the legs are common symptoms.

5. Who is at risk of developing varicose veins?

Risk factors include age, genetics, pregnancy, obesity, standing for long periods, and hormonal changes.
